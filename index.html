// Methodology & Ethics

A structured, repeatable approach grounded in industry standards and ethical responsibility

01

Pre-Engagement & Scoping

Establish clear scope, rules of engagement, and authorization. Define testing windows, out-of-scope systems, and communication protocols. Ensure all legal agreements and liability protections are in place.

02

Intelligence Gathering

Passive and active reconnaissance to map attack surface. Collect information on technologies, endpoints, personnel, and potential entry points while maintaining operational security.

03

Vulnerability Assessment

Systematic identification of security weaknesses using automated scanners, manual testing, and custom tooling. Prioritize findings based on exploitability and business impact.

04

Exploitation

Controlled exploitation of identified vulnerabilities to demonstrate real-world risk. Maintain detailed logs and evidence while avoiding system damage or data corruption.

05

Post-Exploitation & Privilege Escalation

Assess the depth of compromise by attempting lateral movement, privilege escalation, and persistent access. Evaluate the potential impact of a successful attack.

06

Reporting & Remediation

Deliver comprehensive reports with executive summaries and technical details. Include CVSS scoring, proof-of-concept evidence, and prioritized remediation recommendations. Support re-testing post-remediation.
